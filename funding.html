HIV SUCCESS Pilot Program

For our 2024 HIV SUCCESS funding call we will support 2 pilot projects up to $25,000 for early-stage investigators, investigators new to HIV and substance use research or investigators from diverse backgrounds.

Eligible projects should focus on important, new research utilizing cross-cohort data that will lead to future grant applications and discovery to support the heath and well-being of people who use drugs (PWUD) who are at risk for or living with HIV.

RFA release anticipated in late summer or early Fall 2024. Details will be provided here.
