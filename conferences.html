Conferences

AATOD

The American Association for the Treatment of Opioid Dependence

The American Association for the Treatment of Opioid Dependence focuses on disseminating evidence-based initiatives and treatments to help support the health and well-being of people who use drugs and support policy and treatments that make a difference in their lives.

More Information Date: May 18-22, 2024 Location: Las Vegas, Nevada

ASAM

The American Society of Addiction Medicine Conference

More Information Date: April 4-7, 2024 Location: Dallas, Texas Abstract Submission Deadline: October 16, 2023 Abstract Submission

CPDD

College on Problems of Drug Dependence

A national and international forum for scientists of diverse backgrounds to advance the understanding of molecular-neurobiological aspects of addictive disorders.

More Information Date: June 15-19, 2024 Location: Montreal, Canada Abstract Submission Deadline: Open Soon

ISAM

International Society of Addiction Medicine

The International Society of Addiction Medicine Annual Conference focuses on disseminating new research findings, innovative practices, sociopolitical ideas and new approaches to treatment information.

More Information Date: November 2-4, 2023 Location: Marrakesh, Morocco

NAADAC Annual Conference

NAADAC (The Association for Addiction Professionals)

Addiction specific, immersive experience focused on training sessions, education, live question and answer sessions, panel discussions, poster sessions, and more.

More Information Date: October 7-9, 2023 Location: Aurora, CO

NCAD - EAST (East Coast Symposium)

National Conference on Alcohol and Addiction Disorders

The East Coast Symposium, formerly known as NCAD East, brings together like-minded individuals to discuss how to address significant challenges in addiction treatment and learn the latest, most effective strategies for addiction treatment and behavioral health.

More Information Date: November 3-5, 2023 Location: Ponte Vedra Beach, FL

NCAD - WEST (Rocky Mountain Symposium)

National Conference on Alcohol and Addiction Disorders

The West Coast Symposium focuses on addressing significant challenges in addiction treatment. It brings together like-minded peers addiction treatment and behavioral health research and practice to share knowledge and discuss the most effective strategies for addiction treatment.

More Information Date: May 30 - June 1, 2024 Location: Palm Springs, CA

AAAP

American Academy of Addiction Psychiatry

The American Academy of Addiction Psychiatry Annual Meeting focuses on the latest scientific developments in substance use disorders and co-occurring psychiatric disorders.

More Information Date: December 7-10, 2023 Location: San Diego, CA

SBM

Society of Behavioral Medicine

The Society of Behavioral Medicine Annual X brings together scientists, professionals, healthcare providers, government agencies, corporate partners, legislator, non-governmental organizational partners, and students to share cutting edge science and the latest policies and tools to support the best behavioral medicine strategies.

More Information Date: March 13-16, 2024 Location: Philadelphia, PA

IDWeek

Infectious Diseases Society of America

A conference to update infectious diseases professionals, bridge the gaps among clinical practice and research, provide training in skills that promote the Infectious Disease specialty.

More Information Date: October 11-15, 2023 Location: Boston, MA Abstract Submission Deadline: 5/4/2022

IWHOD

International Workshop on HIV and Hepatitis Observational Databases

IWHOD brings senior and junior HIV and hepatitis observational database researchers together to advance the methodology and analysis of observational data.

More Information Date: March 21-23, 2024 Location: Vilamoura, Portugal Abstract Submission: November 3 – December 11, 2024

APHA

American Public Health Association

The American Public Health Association’s Annual Meeting and Expo brings together public health experts and researcher to discuss the latest scientific innovations in public health research.

More Information Date: Nov 12-15, 2023 Location: Atlanta, GA Abstract Submission Deadline: 4/30/2022

SER

Society for Epidemiologic Research

The Annual Meeting of the Society for Epidemiological Research focuses on keeping epidemiologists at the vanguard of scientific developments.

More Information Date: June 18-21, 2024 Location: Austin, TX

CROI

Conference on Retroviruses and Opportunistic Infections

The Conference on Retroviruses and Opportunistic Infections (CROI) provides a forum for basic scientists and clinical investigators to present, discuss, and critique their investigations into the epidemiology and biology of human retroviruses and associated diseases.

More Information Dates: March 3-6, 2024 Venue: Boston, MA Late Breaker Submission Deadline: December 1, 2023

IAS/ AIDS

This biennial conference presents the critical advances in basic, clinical and operational HIV research that move science into policy and practice.

More Information Dates: July 22-26, 2024 Venue: Munich, Germany Abstract Submission Deadline: November 15, 2023- January 23, 2024

ICASA 2023

22nd International Conference in AIDS and STI in Africa

This biennial conference is a major bilingual international AIDS science forum, which alternates between anglophone and francophone African countries.

More Information Dates: December 4-9, 2023 Venue: Harare, Zimbabwe Abstract Submission: Closed

SPR

Society for Prevention Research

Prevention science conference focusing on evidence-based programs policy and science to support health and well-being of all people. The Society for Prevention Research Annual Meeting is a centrally integrated forum for the exchange of new concepts, methods, and results from prevention research. It provides opportunities for scientists, public policy leaders and practitioners to convene and discuss the best strategies for public health.

More Information Dates: May 28-31, 2024 Venue: Washington DC Abstract Submission Deadline: October 31, 2023
