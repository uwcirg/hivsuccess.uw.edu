HIV SUCCESS Conferences

CROI

Conference on Retroviruses and Opportunistic Infections

The Conference on Retroviruses and Opportunistic Infections (CROI) provides a forum for basic scientists and clinical investigators to present, discuss, and critique their investigations into the epidemiology and biology of human retroviruses and associated diseases.

More Information Dates: March 9-12, 2025 Venue: San Francisco, CA

SBM

Society of Behavioral Medicine

The Society of Behavioral Medicine annual meeting brings together scientists, professionals, healthcare providers, government agencies, corporate partners, legislator, non-governmental organizational partners, and students to share cutting edge science and the latest policies and tools to support the best behavioral medicine strategies.

More Information Date: March 26-29, 2025 Location: San Francisco, CA

IWHOD

International Workshop on HIV and Hepatitis Observational DatabasesIWHOD 2025

IWHOD brings senior and junior HIV and hepatitis observational database researchers together to advance the methodology and analysis of observational data.

More Information Date: March 27-29, 2025 Location: Toledo, Spain

ASAM

The American Society of Addiction Medicine Conference

More Information Date: April 24-27, 2025 Location: Denver, CO Abstract Submission Deadline: January 22, 2025 Abstract Submission

ISAM

International Society of Addiction Medicine

The International Society of Addiction Medicine Annual Conference focuses on disseminating new research findings, innovative practices, sociopolitical ideas and new approaches to treatment information.

More Information Date: May 26-28, 2025 Location: Hamburg, Germany

SPR

Society for Prevention Research

Prevention science conference focusing on evidence-based programs policy and science to support health and well-being of all people. The Society for Prevention Research Annual Meeting is a centrally integrated forum for the exchange of new concepts, methods, and results from prevention research. It provides opportunities for scientists, public policy leaders and practitioners to convene and discuss the best strategies for public health.

More Information Dates: May 27-30, 2025 Venue: Seattle, WA

SER

Society for Epidemiologic Research

The Annual Meeting of the Society for Epidemiological Research focuses on keeping epidemiologists at the vanguard of scientific developments.

More Information Date: June 10-13, 2025 Location: Boston, MA

Continuum

International Association of Providers of AIDS Care (IAPAC) hosted conference focusing on evidence, best pratice and implementation of interventions to maximize therapeitic and preventative effects of antiretroviral treatment

More Information Dates: June 10-12, 2025 Location: San Juan, Puerto Rico Abstract Submission Deadline: February 14, 2025

CPDD

College on Problems of Drug Dependence

A national and international forum for scientists of diverse backgrounds to advance the understanding of molecular-neurobiological aspects of addictive disorders.

More Information Date: June 14-18, 2025 Location: New Orleans, LA

IAS/ AIDS

This biennial conference presents the critical advances in basic, clinical and operational HIV research that move science into policy and practice.

More Information Dates: July 13-17, 2025 Location: Kigali, Rwanda Abstract Submission Deadline: January 22, 2025

NAADAC Annual Conference

NAADAC (The Association for Addiction Professionals)

Addiction specific, immersive experience focused on training sessions, education, live question and answer sessions, panel discussions, poster sessions, and more.

More Information Date: October 11-13, 2025 Location: Seattle, WA

IDWeek

Infectious Diseases Society of America

A conference to update infectious diseases professionals, bridge the gaps among clinical practice and research, provide training in skills that promote the Infectious Disease specialty.

More Information Date: October 19-22, 2025 Location: Atlanta, GA

APHA

American Public Health Association

The American Public Health Association’s Annual Meeting and Expo brings together public health experts and researcher to discuss the latest scientific innovations in public health research.

More Information Date: Nov 2-5, 2025 Location: Washington DC

AAAP

American Academy of Addiction Psychiatry

The American Academy of Addiction Psychiatry Annual Meeting focuses on the latest scientific developments in substance use disorders and co-occurring psychiatric disorders.

More Information Date: November 6-9, 2025 Location: San Francisco, CA

ICASA 2023

22nd International Conference in AIDS and STI in Africa

This biennial conference is a major bilingual international AIDS science forum, which alternates between anglophone and francophone African countries.

More Information Dates: December 3-8, 2025 Location: Accra, Ghana
